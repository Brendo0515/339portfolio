Fast Facts about Thomson Reuters!

About

Thomson Reuters was created in 2008 after the Thomson Corporation, an information company active in the fields of financial services, healthcare, law, science and technology research and tax and accounting, merged with the Reuters Group, a multimedia and financial information company. Today, Thomson Reuters employs approximately 45,000 people and operates in over 100 countries.


Using industry expertise and technology, Thomson Reuters provide data and information to leading decision makers in the financial and risk, legal, tax and accounting, and media markets.

Thomson Reuters' legal business serves customers in law firms, corporate legal departments, lawyers and judges, federal and provincial governments, and investigators. Their Tax and Accounting business serves tax & accounting firms, CFOs, CPAs, state and national governments, law firms, global trade organizations, and educational institutions.


Finally, their Media business (Reuters) provides trusted business, financial, national and international news to professionals, the world's media organizations and directly to consumers via reuters.com and Reuters TV. Thomson Reuters also gives back to the community through volunteer events, their employees have committed over 515,499 volunteer hours to their local communities since 2010!
